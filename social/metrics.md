# The Cruise Lab — Social Metrics

Lightweight tracking so the data decides the content (same discipline as the rest of the Lab).
Two rhythms: a **weekly** 10-min jot, a **monthly** 30-min review. Keep it plain — the point is
to spot the top 20% of posts and make more of exactly that, and to see which platform is breaking
out and earns deeper investment.

North-star (goal 1): views/week + follower growth rate per platform.
Leading indicators: 3-sec hook retention %, saves, shares, watch-through %.
Funnel (goals 2–3): link-hub clicks, per-tool referral traffic, affiliate clicks.
