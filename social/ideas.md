# The Cruise Lab — Social Idea Bank

The running list of video/post ideas. Pull from here in each weekly batch (one per pillar,
rotating). Every idea maps to a pillar (P1–P5) and a format (F1–F6, defined in the playbook).
Mark ideas `[done: YYYY-MM-DD → link]` when posted; add new ones freely — the estate is the
content calendar, so this list should only ever grow.

Format key (from playbook): F1 Verdict · F2 Receipt · F3 Myth-Bust · F4 Ranking · F5 Lab Report · F6 Tool Demo (max 1 in 6)
Pillar key: P1 Cabin Verdicts (GCBC) · P2 The Bar Tab (GetMyBarTab) · P3 Connected at Sea (GetMyCruiseConnection) · P4 Port Intel (Shore Thing + GetMyCruiseWeather) · P5 Casino Points & Cruise Maths (Sea You Soon)

## P1 — Cabin Verdicts · series "Cabin Court" (flagship pillar)

1. **The worst cabin on [ship].** (F1) Deck-plan zoom → 3 reasons (above: nightclub / beside: crew door / motion: bow) → BAD stamp. *Repeat per ship — endless.*
2. **The best-value balcony on [ship].** (F1) Not the priciest — the one that scores highest per pound. Show the score, show the location.
3. **Never book a cabin directly under the pool deck. Here's what's above you.** (F3/F1) Deck-plan cross-section reveal of the pool/Lido right over the "quiet" cabin.
4. **We scored ~70,000 cabins. These 3 mistakes come up on every ship.** (F5) Under-the-gym, above-the-theatre, beside-the-crew-door — one deck-plan example each.
5. **Guarantee cabin roulette: what the cruise line can actually give you.** (F3) Range of best-to-worst you could be assigned, scored, so viewers see the gamble.
6. **Connecting-cabin trap: the "quiet" cabin that shares a wall with a family suite.** (F1) Deck-plan highlight + why the score drops.

## P2 — The Bar Tab · series "The Tab" (GetMyBarTab)

1. **Is the drinks package worth it? We did the maths.** (F2) Itemised tab prints line by line → total vs package → verdict.
2. **The break-even is ~6 drinks a day. Here's what 6 drinks actually looks like.** (F2/F5) Visual stack of a realistic 6-drink day → the number.
3. **Two coffees and a cocktail: the "I'm barely drinking" tab that still beats the package.** (F2) The light-drinker scenario people get wrong.
4. **The 18% service charge nobody budgets for.** (F3) How gratuity quietly changes the package break-even.
5. **Sea day vs port day: your bar spend is not the same.** (F5) Split the tab — why port days cut drink spend and shift the maths.
6. **Mocktails, water, specialty coffee: what the package does and doesn't cover.** (F3) The line items that surprise people.

## P3 — Connected at Sea · series "Lab Report" (GetMyCruiseConnection — myth-bust gold)

1. **Your travel eSIM will NOT work at sea. Here's why.** (F3) Land-network-only explained in 15s → the two that do work.
2. **The £300 airplane-mode horror bill — and the setting that prevents it.** (F3) The one toggle people forget.
3. **Only two eSIMs actually work in open ocean.** (F5) GigSky (Cellular at Sea) + RedBull Mobile Maritime (Telenor) — check the app before you buy.
4. **Starlink changed cruise WiFi. When ship WiFi is now the right call.** (F3/F5) Sea-day recommendation flipped.
5. **Port day vs sea day connectivity: two totally different games.** (F5) What works where, in one graphic.
6. **Airalo vs Holafly vs ship WiFi: which for which day.** (F4) Ranking by scenario, not by brand.
