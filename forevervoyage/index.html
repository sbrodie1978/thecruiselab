Questions

How do I get my photos to you?

Whatever's easiest for you — a shared phone album, a Google Photos or iCloud link, a Dropbox or WeTransfer folder, or even a memory stick in the post. Once you get in touch I'll point you to the simplest option for your setup.

Who can see my site?

Only the people you share the link with. Forever Voyage sites aren't listed anywhere, aren't searchable on Google, and carry no adverts or tracking. It's private by default — yours to keep close or share as you wish.

How long does it stay online?

Your site is built to last and kept online so you can revisit it for years. If anything ever needs to change, you can just get in touch.

Whose cruise is the example?

My own — a trip I took with my mum around the Norwegian fjords. I've shared it as the sample with her blessing, with our names changed and faces blurred. Your own site is never used as an example without your explicit permission.

How long does it take to build?

Usually a week or so from the point I have your photos and notes, depending on how much there is. Bespoke voyages take a little longer. You'll always see a preview and sign it off before it goes live.
